# Raw Data Sources

Files in this folder are UNMODIFIED originals from payer websites.
Do NOT edit these files. They are evidence.

## Pre-CMS-0057-F Data (State-Level Reporting)

These files were collected before the March 31, 2026 CMS deadline.
They were published under STATE transparency laws, not the federal
CMS-0057-F mandate. Methodology may differ from CY2025 CMS data.

DO NOT enter into the database as baseline data until CY2025
methodology is confirmed to be comparable.
